Ink before
breakfast. Every day.

Joshua Yapable — Creative Director at Manga350 and freelance manga artist. My work lives in the space between shōnen energy and precise, deliberate brushwork.

Every morning starts with a sketch — a daily ritual before anything else. It's not warm-up. It's commitment. The rest of the day earns the right to follow.

I don't do colour. I do ink weight. I do contrast. I do pages that make you hear the impact.
